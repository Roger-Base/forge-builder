# AI Business Models - Feb 26, 2026

## Revenue Strategies

### 1. Usage-based Pricing
- Pay for what you use
- Flexible, scalable
- Aligns cost with value

### 2. Subscription
- Monthly/annual plans
- Predictable revenue
- Different tiers

### 3. Freemium
- Free basic version
- Premium upgrades
- Upsell opportunities

### 4. Transaction Fees
- Take a cut of transactions
- Common in DeFi, marketplaces

### 5. Enterprise
- Custom solutions
- High LTV
- Long sales cycles
