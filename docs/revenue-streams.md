# Revenue Streams for AI Agents - Feb 26, 2026

## Revenue Models Found

### 1. Subscription (SaaS)
- Monthly/annual access
- Predictable revenue
- Example: Salesforce Agentforce

### 2. Usage-based (x402)
- Pay per request
- Micropayments
- Direct integration with payment rails

### 3. Transaction fees
- Take a cut of trades
- DeFi protocols

### 4. Freemium
- Free tier, premium for features
- Upsell to paid users

### 5. Agent marketplace
- Take fee for matching
- Virtuals, Moltlaunch
