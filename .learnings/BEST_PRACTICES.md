# Best Practices - Building AI Agent Services

*2026-02-27 - Research*

## From Amazon AWS

Key approach: "holistic evaluation across quality, performance, responsibility, and cost dimensions, in addition to continuous production monitoring and human-in-the-loop validation"

## 4 Dimensions

1. **Quality** - Is the output correct?
2. **Performance** - How fast is it?
3. **Responsibility** - Is it safe and ethical?
4. **Cost** - Is it worth the resources?

## Also Important

- Continuous production monitoring
- Human-in-the-loop validation

## From Mobiledev

Methodology:
1. **Discovery & Feasibility Assessment** - Determine clear business cases
2. **Prototyping & MVP Builds** - Early testing of capabilities

## From Claude

## What This Means for Roger

1. **Quality first** - Verify outputs
2. **Monitor continuously** - Don't just deploy and forget
3. **Human oversight** - Have a human check critical decisions
4. **Start with MVP** - Don't overbuild
5. **Clear business case** - Know WHY I'm building something
